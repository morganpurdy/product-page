Keurig K-Elite coffeemaker

The newest Keurig® single serve coffee maker, the Keurig® K-Elite™ brewer blends a premium finish and programmable features to deliver both modern design and the ultimate in beverage customization. With a striking brushed finish and metal details, it’s a stylish addition to any kitchen.

Features

Premium finish.

The K-Elite™ coffee maker delivers modern design and the ultimate in beverage customization.

Bold flavor.

Increase the strength and bold taste of your coffee with STRONG brew. Choose from 5 cup sizes.

Iced Setting.

Brew hot over ice at the touch of a button for full-flavored, delicious iced coffee.

Details

The Keurig® K-Elite™ coffee maker features Strong Brew for when you want to brew a bolder cup of coffee, and an Iced button to brew hot over ice for a refreshing, full-flavored iced coffee. It features five brew sizes, so you can brew 4, 6, 8, 10, or 12oz of your favorite coffee, tea, hot cocoa, or iced beverage at the touch of a button. Choose from hundreds of delicious varieties of K-Cup® pods, or brew your own ground coffee using the Universal My K-Cup® Reusable Coffee Filter (sold separately).

Additional info:

Coffee in no time: Brews K-Cup® pods in under a minute.

Large 75oz water Reservoir: Allows you to brew 8 cups before having to refill.

Hot water on demand: Perfect for instant soups or oatmeal.

Brewer maintenance reminder: Alerts you when it is time to descale your coffee maker.

Quiet brew technology™: Minimizes noise when the coffee maker is in use.
